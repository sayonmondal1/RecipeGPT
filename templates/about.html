About RecipeGPT

The RecipeGPT is a smart recipe generation application that helps you discover delicious recipes based on the ingredients you already have at home. No more wasting time searching endlessly—just enter your ingredients, and our system suggests quick, healthy, and creative meal ideas!

Designed for food lovers, students, and busy people, this project reduces food waste, saves time, and makes cooking fun again.
